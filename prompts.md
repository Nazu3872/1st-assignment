## Journey Section Prompt

Create a modern "Journey" section for a developer conference landing page.

Add a centered heading titled "Start Your DevConf Journey" with a short subtitle: "Join inspiring sessions, connect with experts, and grow your career at DevConf 2026."

Below the heading, create four horizontal cards connected with blue arrow icons.

Card 1:
- Step: 1
- Icon: 🎟️
- Title: Register
- Description: Reserve your seat and receive your conference pass instantly.

Card 2:
- Step: 2
- Icon: 📝
- Title: Check-In
- Description: Collect your badge, welcome kit, and event schedule.

Card 3:
- Step: 3
- Icon: 🎤
- Title: Learn
- Description: Attend inspiring keynotes and hands-on technical workshops.

Card 4:
- Step: 4
- Icon: 🤝
- Title: Connect
- Description: Build relationships with developers, founders, and recruiters.

Use a clean white background, rounded cards with subtle borders, centered content, modern typography, equal spacing between cards, blue circular step badges, and make the layout fully responsive.
